Frequently Asked Questions

What is paperboard?
Paperboard (also known as folding cartons) is an excellent cost-effective, and consumer-friendly option for packaging various products. Folding carton boxes are flexible and compatible with many printing options and new processes. At Marvel Print Studio, we can provide several customizable paperboard box materials such as white paperboard, Craft, food-grade, CCNB, and SBS paperboard.
